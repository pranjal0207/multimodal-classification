Spinach Artichoke Mac &amp; Cheese Recipe by Tasty
Dinner
Here's what you need: butter, garlic, spinach, artichoke heart, milk, kosher salt, black pepper, elbow macaroni, shredded cheddar cheese, shredded mozzarella cheese
American
2 tablespoons butter 2 cloves garlic, minced 5 ounces spinach 1 cup artichoke hearts, drained 3 cups milk 2 teaspoons kosher salt 2 teaspoons freshly ground black pepper 2 cups elbow macaroni ¾ cup shredded cheddar cheese 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). In a large pan, melt the butter. Add garlic, and spinach cooking until spinach is wilted. Add artichoke hearts. Slowly add the milk, salt, and pepper. Stir until milk is boiling. Add the macaroni, cooking until the milk coats the macaroni like a glaze. Add the cheddar and 2 cups (200g) of mozzarella, stirring until the cheese is completely melted. Sprinkle the remaining mozzarella on top. Bake for 7 minutes, until cheese is bubbly and golden brown. Enjoy!
