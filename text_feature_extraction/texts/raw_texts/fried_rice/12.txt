Sweet Potato &quot;Fried&quot; Rice Recipe by Tasty
Sides
Here's what you need: medium-sized sweet potato, cooking oil, white onion, large carrot, salt, pepper, vegetable broth, pea, egg, low sodium soy sauce, green onion
Fusion
2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and chopped 1 tablespoon preferred cooking oil or water ½ white onion, diced 2 large carrots, peeled and sliced ½ teaspoon salt ½ teaspoon pepper ½ cup vegetable broth ½ cup peas, cooked 2 eggs, scrambled 2 tablespoons low sodium soy sauce Green onions, chopped, for garnish
In a food processor, pulse the sweet potato chunks until they reach your desired “rice” consistency. In a large skillet over medium heat, heat oil, then add onions and let cook until translucent. Add sweet potato rice, carrots, salt, pepper, and vegetable broth and cook until the liquid has evaporated and the sweet potato is tender, stirring occasionally. Add peas, eggs, and soy sauce, and combine, allowing it to heat through. Garnish with green onions. Enjoy!
