Strawberry Kiwi Frozen Yogurt Recipe by Tasty
Desserts
Here's what you need: frozen strawberry, kiwi, greek yogurt, honey
American
3 cups frozen strawberries 3 kiwis, peeled and sliced 1 cup Greek yogurt 3 tablespoons honey or agave
Combine all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Transfer to an 8x8-inch (20x20 cm) glass dish. Freeze for at least 2 hours. Enjoy!
