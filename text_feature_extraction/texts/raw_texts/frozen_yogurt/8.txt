Tropical Frozen Yogurt Recipe by Tasty
Desserts
Here's what you need: frozen mango, pineapple, banana, plain yogurt, honey
American
1 cup frozen mango 1 cup pineapple 2 bananas 1 cup plain yogurt 3 tablespoons honey
Combine all ingredients in a food processor until smooth. Transfer to an 8x8-inch (20x20 cm) glass dish. Freeze for at least 2 hours. Enjoy!
