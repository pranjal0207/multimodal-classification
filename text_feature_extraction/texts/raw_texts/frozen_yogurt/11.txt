Yogurt Granola Frozen Banana Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: finger banana, vanilla yogurt, granola, popsicle stick
American
1 10 finger bushel of bananas 1 cup vanilla yogurt 1 cup granola 20 popsicle sticks
Peel bananas and cut in half.  Place popsicle sticks inside bananas and put on tray with parchment paper. Freeze for 1 to 3 hours depending on how firm you want your bananas to be. When bananas are ready, coat in yogurt and sprinkle with granola. Set aside and freeze for an additional 20 minutes. Enjoy!
