Strawberry Banana Frozen Yogurt Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: strawberry, banana, plain greek yogurt, fresh strawberry 
American
3 cups frozen strawberries 3 frozen bananas 2/3 cup plain Greek yogurt Chopped fresh strawberries (optional)
Combine frozen fruit and yogurt in a food processor. Blend until smooth. Spread mixture onto an 8x8 (20 x 20cm) glass baking dish, and sprinkle on fresh strawberries. Cover and freeze for 2 hours. Enjoy!
