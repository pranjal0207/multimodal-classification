Waffle Omelette Recipe by Tasty
Breakfast
Here's what you need: egg, salt, pepper, nonstick cooking spray

2 eggs, beaten Salt, to taste Pepper, to taste Nonstick cooking spray
In a medium bowl, add the beaten eggs, any of your favorite toppings, salt, and pepper. Mix well. Grease a waffle iron with nonstick spray.  Add the egg mixture and cook according to machine instruction to until the eggs are fully set and slightly browned, 2 minutes. Remove the egg from the waffle iron and serve. Enjoy!
