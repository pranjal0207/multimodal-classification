Pizza Waffles Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: pizza dough, marinara sauce, shredded mozzarella cheese, pepperoni
Fusion
1 (14-ounce) tube pizza dough or homemade pizza dough 4 tablespoons marinara sauce 1 cup shredded mozzarella 12 slices pepperoni
Slice the pizza dough into 8 equal pieces, then press each piece flat. Place 4 pieces of dough into a preheated, greased waffle iron. Spread one tablespoon of marinara, Â¼ cup (25 grams) of mozzarella, and 3 slices of pepperoni on each piece of dough. Top with the 4 remaining pieces of dough and seal. Cook for 3-5 minutes, until golden brown, then serve. Enjoy!
