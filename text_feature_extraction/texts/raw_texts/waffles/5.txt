Ham &amp; Cheese Panini Waffle Recipe by Tasty
Breakfast
Here's what you need: sourdough bread, egg, fresh chives, ham, provolone cheese

2 slices sourdough bread 3 eggs, scrambled 1 tablespoon chives, chopped 2 slices ham 3 slices provolone
Place a slice of bread in a preheated, greased waffle iron. Spread the eggs evenly on top. Sprinkle the chives over the eggs. Top with the ham, followed by the cheese. Place the second slice of bread on top. Cook for 3-5 minutes, until cheese is melted and the bread is golden brown, then serve. Enjoy!
