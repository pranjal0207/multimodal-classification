Paleo Chocolate Mousse Recipe by Tasty
Desserts
Here's what you need: unsweetened chocolate, coconut cream, all natural coconut milk, unprocessed organic honey, pure vanilla extract
American
6 ounces unsweetened chocolate, cubed 12 ounces additive/BPA-free organic coconut cream, refrigerated overnight 4 ounces all natural coconut milk â…“ cup unprocessed organic honey 1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract
In a small microwave-safe bowl, microwave chocolate on medium-high heat in 30-second intervals, stirring in between, until chocolate is melted. Let cool. In a medium-size bowl, add the coconut cream, coconut milk, honey, and vanilla. Whisk until incorporated. Fold in the melted chocolate. Divide evenly among ramekins. Refrigerate for 1 hour. Garnish to your liking. Enjoy!
