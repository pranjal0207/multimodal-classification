In a medium-sized bowl, beat aquafaba until stiff peaks form. Gently fold in melted chocolate, sugar, and vanilla, being careful not to overmix. Distribute mousse into 3 cups and chill for at least 3 hours, ideally overnight. Garnish with fresh strawberries or fruit of choice. Enjoy!
