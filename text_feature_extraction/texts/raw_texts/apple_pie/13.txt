Slow-Cooker Apple Spice Cobbler Recipe by Tasty
Desserts
Here's what you need: apple pie filling, spice cake mix, butter, vanilla ice cream
American
2  20z cans Apple Pie Filling 1 Box Spice Cake Mix 8oz Butter n/a
Combine the Apple Pie filling, cake mix, and butter in the slow cooker Bake on High for 2 to 2.5 hours Server a la mode with vanilla ice cream or your favorite whipped cream. Enjoy!
