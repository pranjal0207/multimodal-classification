Apple Slab Pie Recipe by Tasty
Desserts
Here's what you need: granny smith apples, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, salt, orange zest, refrigerated pie crust
American
5 cups Granny Smith apples, chopped (about 8 medium-sized apples) ¾ cup sugar 3 tablespoons cornstarch 2 teaspoons cinnamon Pinch of salt Zest of 1 orange 4 8-inch refrigerated pie crusts
Preheat oven to 400°F (200˚C). Peel, core, and cut apples into ½-inch (1 ¼ cm) chunks. In a bowl, mix the chopped apples, sugar, cornstarch, cinnamon, salt, and orange zest. Set aside. On parchment paper, stack 2 pie crusts on top of each other. Roll out to 17 inches by 12 inches (43 cm by 30 cm). Cut diagonally into ¾-inch (2 cm) strips. Roll out another 2 pie crusts. Place on a 15 x 10 x 1 inch ( 38 x  26 x 3 cm) baking sheet. Spread out apple mixture into the crust, then form a lattice top with the pie crust strips.  Tuck the crust inward around the edges, and bake for 35-40 minutes at 400°F (200˚C). Serve warm with ice cream. Enjoy!
