One-pan Flank Steak &amp; Veggies Recipe by Tasty
Dinner
Here's what you need: broccoli floret, white onion, flank steak, olive oil, wild rice, salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, onion powder
American
12 ounces broccoli florets ½ white onion, sliced 1 1½-pound flank steak 2 tablespoons olive oil 4 cups cooked wild rice, to serve 1½  teaspoons salt 1½ teaspoons pepper 1 teaspoons garlic powder 1 teaspoons paprika 1 teaspoons onion powder
Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). In a small bowl, add the salt, pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and onion powder. Stir until well combined. Place broccoli, onions, and flank steak on a baking tray. Coat broccoli and onions with olive oil. Sprinkle seasoning mix on flank steak, broccoli, and onions until coated. Bake for 15 minutes. Broil for 2 minutes. For medium, internal temperature of steak should reach 145°F (65°C). Let steak rest 10 minutes before cutting. Serve now with wild rice or refrigerate in airtight containers up to 3-4 days. Enjoy!
