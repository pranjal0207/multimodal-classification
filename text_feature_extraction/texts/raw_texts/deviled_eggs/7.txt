Halloween Pumpkin Deviled Eggs Recipe by Tasty
Appetizers
Here's what you need: hard-boiled egg, dijon mustard, mayonnaise, paprika, salt, pepper, fresh chives
Fusion
6 hard-boiled eggs 3 tablespoons dijon mustard Â¼ cup mayonnaise 1 teaspoon paprika plus additional for garnish Salt, to taste Pepper, to taste Chives for garnish, finely chopped
Peel and slice hard boiled eggs lengthwise. Separate yolks into a separate bowl. Add mustard, mayonnaise, paprika, salt, and pepper to the yolks, and mix thoroughly. Fill a piping bag with the yolk mixture, and pipe into the empty egg whites. Sprinkle with paprika, and place a piece of chive to make it look like a pumpkin. Enjoy!
