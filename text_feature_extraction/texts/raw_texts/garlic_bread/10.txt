Tomato Mozzarella Garlic Bread Dippers Recipe by Tasty
Appetizers
Here's what you need: bread, garlic, butter, mozzarella cheese, large tomato, fresh basil, salt, pepper, italian seasoning
Fusion
1 loaf of bread (preferably ciabatta) 3 cloves of garlic, minced 4 Tbsp. butter 12 oz mozzarella cheese, sliced 2 large tomatoes, sliced 1/4 cup basil, chopped salt and pepper to taste n/a n/a
Soften the butter and mix it with the minced garlic. Cut the loaf of bread in half lengthwise. Spread the garlic butter on the bread. Top with mozzarella, and bake at 400°F (205°C) for 15 minutes.  Top with tomatoes and basil, sprinkle on salt and pepper. For the dipping sauce, mix equal parts of balsamic vinegar and olive oil, and sprinkle with Italian seasoning. Slice into strips. Enjoy!
