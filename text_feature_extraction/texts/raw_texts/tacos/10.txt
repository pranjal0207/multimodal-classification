Vegetarian Black Bean And Sweet Potato Tacos Recipe by Tasty
Lunch
Here's what you need: sweet potato, olive oil, kosher salt, ground black pepper, corn tortilla, black beans, pico de gallo, avocado, fresh cilantro

¼ cup diced sweet potato (⅓ of a sweet potato) 1 teaspoon olive oil ½ teaspoon kosher salt ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper 2 corn tortillas ¼ cup canned black beans, drained and rinsed ¼ cup pico de gallo (see recipe) ¼ of an avocado, diced 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
Toss the sweet potatoes with the olive oil, salt, and pepper in a medium microwave-safe bowl, and microwave until tender, about 2 minutes. Heat the tortillas in a dry skillet over low heat and fill with cooked sweet potatoes, black the beans, pico de gallo, and avocado. Garnish with cilantro. Enjoy!
