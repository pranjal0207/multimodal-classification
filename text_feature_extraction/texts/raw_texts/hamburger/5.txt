French Onion Burger Recipe by Tasty
Dinner
Here's what you need: ground beef, salt, pepper, oil, hamburger bun, comté cheese, caramelized onion, fresh arugula, mustard
French
1 pound ground beef, 80% lean 1 teaspoon salt 1 teaspoon pepper 1 tablespoon oil 3 hamburger buns 3 ounces comté cheese, sliced 1 cup caramelized onions 1 cup arugula n/a
Mix together the patty ingredients until evenly combined. Form the beef into a round patty. Repeat with the remaining beef. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the burgers for 3 minutes on the first side, flip, and place the slice of comté on top. Cook for 6 more minutes. Assemble the burgers on the buns with caramelized onions and arugula. Enjoy!
