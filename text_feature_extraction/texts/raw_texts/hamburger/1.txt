American Burger “Juicy Lucy” Recipe by Tasty
Dinner
Here's what you need: ground beef, salt, pepper, onion powder, oil, cheddar cheese, hamburger bun, bacon, tomato, lettuce
American
1 pound ground beef, 80% lean 1 teaspoon salt ½ teaspoon pepper 1 teaspoon onion powder 1 tablespoon oil 2 ounces cheddar cheese, sliced into 3 square chunks 3 hamburger buns 3 strips bacon, cooked and sliced in half 3 slices tomato 3 pieces lettuce
Mix together the patty ingredients until evenly combined. Flatten the beef into a thin layer, then place a chunk of cheddar inside, wrapping the beef completely around the cheddar. Form a patty with your hands. Repeat with the remaining beef and cheese. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Cook the burgers for 3 minutes on the first side, flip, then cook for 6 more minutes. Assemble the burgers with the bun, bacon, tomato, and lettuce. Enjoy!
