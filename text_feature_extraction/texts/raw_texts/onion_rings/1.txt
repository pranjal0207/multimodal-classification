BBQ Bacon Onion Rings Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: onion, bacon, BBQ sauce, honey
BBQ
2 onions 20 slices bacon ½ cup BBQ sauce ¼ cup honey
Preheat oven to 400˚F (200˚C). Peel and cut onions into ½-inch (1 cm) rings. Separate the rings. Wrap a piece of bacon around one onion ring. Repeat with remaining onion rings and place on a parchment paper-lined baking sheet. Combine the BBQ sauce and honey in a bowl, and brush onto rings. Bake for 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Enjoy!
