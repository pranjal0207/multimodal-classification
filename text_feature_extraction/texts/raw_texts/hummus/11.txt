Veggie Hummus Trio Recipe by Tasty
Appetizers
Here's what you need: hummus, small avocado, red pepper, beet, pita chip
Fusion
24 ounces hummus, divided 1 small avocado, sliced 3 ounces roasted red peppers Â½ cup canned beets Pita chips, for serving
In a food processor, combine 8 ounces (225 g) of hummus with the avocado. Puree until smooth. Transfer to a small bowl. Clean the food processor. Add 8 ounces (225 g) of hummus and the red peppers. Puree until smooth. Transfer to another small bowl. Clean the food processor. Add the remaining hummus and the beets. Puree until smooth. Transfer to another small bowl. Serve with pita chips. Enjoy!
