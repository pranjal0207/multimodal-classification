Savory Pumpkin Hummus Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: chickpeas, pumpkin puree, olive oil, garlic, salt, paprika, fresh rosemary, lemon juice, pita chip

1, 15-ounce can chickpeas, drained and rinsed 1 cup pumpkin puree 2 tablespoons olive oil 2 cloves garlic, minced Salt, to taste ½ teaspoon paprika ½ teaspoon rosemary ¼ cup lemon juice Pita chips, to serve
Combine chickpeas, pumpkin, olive oil, garlic, salt, paprika, rosemary and lemon juice in a food processor or blender, and blend until smooth. Add more olive oil or water as necessary to reach desired consistency. Serve with pita chips. Enjoy!
