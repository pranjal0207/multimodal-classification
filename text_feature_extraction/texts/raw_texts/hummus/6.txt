Garlic And Herb Hummus Recipe by Tasty
Appetizers
Here's what you need: garbanzo beans, garlic, tahini, lemon, olive oil, salt, pine nuts, fresh parsley, water, pita chip
Middle Eastern
garbanzo beans 1 15-oz can garlic 2-3 cloves tahini 3 Tbsp. juice of 1 lemon olive oil 2-3 Tbsp. salt to taste pine nuts as a garnish fresh parsley about 1 1/2 Tbsp. water 2-3 Tbsp. n/a
Boil the garbanzo beans until the skins come off. Remove the skins, drain. Combine all ingredients in a food processor. Blend until smooth adding small amounts of oil and water until desired consistency is achieved. Add more lemon or tahini if needed. Garnish with pine nuts and more oil! Eat with fresh vegetables or pita chips. Enjoy!
