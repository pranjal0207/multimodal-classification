Microwave 4-minute BBQ Chicken Nachos Recipe by Tasty
Snacks
Here's what you need: rotisserie chicken, BBQ sauce, tortilla chips, pico de gallo, shredded cheddar cheese, green onion, guacamole, sour cream
Fusion
1 cup shredded rotisserie chicken 3 tablespoons barbecue sauce 2 large handfuls tortilla chips ¼ cup pico de gallo 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese ¼ cup scallions, thinly sliced Guacamole, to serve Sour cream, to serve
Mix the chicken with the barbecue sauce, then spread evenly over a bed of tortilla chips. Spread the pico de gallo, cheese, and green onions evenly on top, then microwave for about 1-2 minutes until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Dollop with some guacamole and sour cream, then serve. Enjoy!
