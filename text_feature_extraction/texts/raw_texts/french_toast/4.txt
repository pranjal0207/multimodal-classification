Blueberry French Toast In A Mug Recipe by Tasty
Breakfast
Here's what you need: bread, egg, milk, cinnamon, vanilla extract, blueberry, maple syrup
American
Slice of bread 1 egg 2 tablespoons milk ¼ teaspoon cinnamon ½ teaspoon vanilla extract Blueberries Maple syrup
Slice bread into cubes. In a bowl, add egg, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla, and mix. In a microwave-safe mug, place half of the cubed bread and desired amount of blueberries. Place the rest of the bread, berries, and the egg mixture into the mug. Microwave for 1½ minutes or until egg is fully cooked. Drizzle maple syrup. Enjoy!
