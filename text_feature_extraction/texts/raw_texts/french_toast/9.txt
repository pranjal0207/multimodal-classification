Nutella Marshmallow French Toast Recipe by Tasty
Breakfast
Here's what you need: chocolate hazelnut spread, marshmallow, white bread, egg, double cream, butter
Fusion
Nutella Marshmallows White bread 2 eggs 200ml double cream Butter (for frying)
Spread nutella on both slices of white bread. Cut marshmallows in half and place on one side of the bread. Close sandwich. Whisk together 2 eggs and the double cream. Dip nutella marshmallow sandwich in mixture. Fry in butter until both sides are golden brown. Enjoy!
